Burns

Steps to take in case of minor and major burns.

Types of Burns

Burns are classified into three types:

  • First-degree burns: Affect the outer layer of skin (epidermis)
  • Second-degree burns: Affect both the outer layer and the underlying layer (dermis)
  • Third-degree burns: Affect the deeper tissues, including muscles and bones

Treatment

Steps to treat burns:

  • Cool the burn with running water for at least 10 minutes
  • Cover the burn with a sterile, non-stick bandage
  • Avoid using ice, butter, or ointments on the burn
  • Take over-the-counter pain relievers if needed
  • Seek medical attention for severe burns
