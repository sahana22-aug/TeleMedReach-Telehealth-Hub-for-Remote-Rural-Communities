Learn More About TeleMedReach

TeleMedReach is an open-source telehealth platform designed to provide remote healthcare services for remote communities. It offers the following features:

  • TeleHealth: Connect with doctors via video, chat, or audio calls.
  • Health Education: Access a library of health education resources.
  • Health Schemes: Information on various government health schemes.
  • Latest Health News: Stay updated with the latest health news and updates.
