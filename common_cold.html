Common Cold

Information on symptoms, prevention, and treatment of the common cold.

Symptoms

Common symptoms of the common cold include:

  • Runny nose
  • Sore throat
  • Cough
  • Congestion
  • Sneezing
  • Low-grade fever
  • Body aches

Prevention

Tips to prevent the common cold:

  • Wash hands frequently with soap and water
  • Avoid close contact with sick individuals
  • Disinfect frequently-touched surfaces
  • Maintain a healthy lifestyle to boost your immune system
  • Cover your mouth with hankis
  • Avoid sharing cups, utensils, and personal items
  • Probiotics can support gut health and immunity

Home Remedies

Home remedies to alleviate symptoms of the common cold:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of fluids
  • Get plenty of rest
  • Gargle with salt water to soothe a sore throat
  • Use a humidifier to keep the air moist
  • Drink warm liquids like tea with honey
  • Take over-the-counter medications to relieve symptoms
