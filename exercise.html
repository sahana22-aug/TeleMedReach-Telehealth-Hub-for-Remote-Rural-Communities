Nutrition & Exercise

Learn about balanced diets, vitamins, minerals, and the benefits of regular physical activity.

Balanced Diet

A balanced diet includes a variety of foods in the right proportions to provide the nutrients your body needs.

  • Fruits and vegetables
  • Whole grains
  • Proteins (meat, fish, eggs, beans)
  • Dairy products
  • Fats and oils

Vitamins and Minerals

Vitamins and minerals are essential nutrients that your body needs to function properly.

  • Vitamin A: Important for vision and immune function.
  • Vitamin C: Helps with the growth and repair of tissues.
  • Calcium: Essential for strong bones and teeth.
  • Iron: Necessary for the production of red blood cells.

Exercise

Discover the benefits of regular physical activity.

Benefits of Exercise

Regular physical activity can improve your muscle strength and boost your endurance.

  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Helps with weight management
  • Reduces the risk of chronic diseases
  • Improves mental health and mood
  • Strengthens bones and muscles

Types of Exercise

There are various types of exercises that you can incorporate into your routine.

  • Aerobic exercises: Running, swimming, cycling
  • Strength training: Weight lifting, resistance bands
  • Flexibility exercises: Yoga, stretching
  • Balance exercises: Tai Chi, balance drills
